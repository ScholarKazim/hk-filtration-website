Industrial Hydraulic Filter Element

Model: MF1065.1115.10.15

The MF1065.1115.10.15 is a premium industrial grade hydraulic filter element engineered for placement in high-pressure hydraulic circuits. Constructed with premium inorganic glass fiber media, it guarantees stable filtration performance under varying pressures.

Micron Rating: 10 Micron Absolute (Beta 10 ≥ 1000)
Media: Inorganic Glass Fiber
Flow Direction: Out-to-In
Request Quote View Specifications

Materials & Construction

Filter media: Glass fiber. Support layer: Stainless steel wire mesh. End caps: Zinc-plated steel. O-rings: Viton (FKM).

The structural reinforcement of the internal core prevents collapse during pressure spikes up to 210 bar. Premium Viton seals provide leakage protection under aggressive chemical fluids and high temperatures.

Compatible Equipment

Direct replacement element for HYDAC, Parker, Bosch Rexroth housings, heavy excavators, power generators, steel mills, and marine steering systems.

Built according to strict ISO standardization parameters. Matches fit, form, and function specs of leading original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
